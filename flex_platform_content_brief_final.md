## Features



## Portfolio

Manage your entire energy portfolio in one place.
Monitor all systems, identify issues, and prioritise action.

**Value:** Focus effort where it has the biggest impact.


**Example:** 
A multi-site business identifies an underperforming site and improves efficiency by aligning operations with solar generation.


---



## Energy Dashboard

Understand how energy flows and what it costs.
See generation, consumption, import, and export in real time.

**Value:** Make decisions that reduce energy costs.


**Example:** 
Shifting operations into solar hours reduces grid import and daily costs.


---



## Load Monitoring

Stay within your site’s limits and avoid downtime.
Track real-time load and receive alerts.

**Value:** Prevent outages and avoid costly repairs.


**Example:** 
An alert helps avoid exceeding capacity and prevents a blown fuse.


---



## Scheduling

Take control of how your energy is used.
Schedule battery charge and discharge.

**Value:** Maximise battery ROI and reduce costs.


**Example:** 
Charging during solar and discharging at peak pricing reduces grid costs.


---



## Flex:Assure

Ensure your system is delivering what was promised.
Compare actual performance against forecast.

**Value:** Validate ROI and detect issues early.


**Example:** 
Underperformance is identified and corrected, restoring expected output.
